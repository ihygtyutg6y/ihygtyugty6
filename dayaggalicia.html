Most people know that certain types of waste need to be disposed of in specific ways, in order to keep from contaminating the environment through improper waste disposal.

Root Cause of the Behavior

The country's open dump sites are one of the major reasons why waste product or waste materials leaked to the waterways that's why water became affected and get contaminated.

Problem to be Solve

The general objective of our study is to teach the residents of any Brgys on how to properly dispose their garbage, to find alternative ways in eliminating their garbage and to ensure that the community will maintain the cleanliness in any Brgys

Possible Solution

Solving these waste disposal problems requires a large-scale effort directly from the source, meaning manufacturers need to take responsibility for their waste by operating in a circular economy as much as possible. However, individuals can also play a role in the circular economy by seeking to fix products they currently own rather than throw them out.

Significance of the Study
